About me: family.
In that order.

I was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah. I attended Catholic school most my life. After graduating from Judge Memorial in 2000, I moved to Tempe, AZ where I completed both my undergrad degrees and my MBA at Arizona State University. I holds an MBA in International Business Management and dual bachelors in Chemistry and Business Management.

In 2006 I moved back to Utah to start my first company Alpine Auto Transporters, a transportation software company that provided car dealerships and auctions nationwide with real time tracking for all their vehicles bought and sold outside their home states. After partnering with eBay Motors for a number of years, I went on to sell the company to DealerTrack in 2013. Since then, I have had my hand in countless projects, founding multiple companies across a wide variety of industries, and in 2013 my accomplishments were recognized by Utah Business Magazine. In February 2013 I was a recipient of their 40 Under 40 Award

I now resides in Draper, UT with his wife Natalie, our twins Nomi and Kingston, and our Frenchie Steve. I enjoys golf and pretty much any activity that requires being active and spending time with my family.
